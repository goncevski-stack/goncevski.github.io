Privacy Policy

Arrow Slide  ·  Last updated: March 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Arrow Slide ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Arrow Slide does not directly collect any personally identifiable information from users. However, third-party services integrated into the app may collect certain information automatically, including:

• Device information (device type, operating system version)
• Advertising ID
• App usage data and interactions
• IP address

2. Advertising

Arrow Slide uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads based on your interests. This includes the use of advertising identifiers on your device.

For more information on how Google uses data from apps that use AdMob, please visit: policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings under Google Settings → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

3. Data Storage

Arrow Slide stores game progress data (such as completed levels and scores) locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

4. Children's Privacy

Arrow Slide is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

• Google AdMob — Advertising (Privacy Policy)
• Google Play Services — App distribution (Privacy Policy)

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
